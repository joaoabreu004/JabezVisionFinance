WHAT IS A VISION BOARD?

"A vision board is a collage of pictures and phrases that depict things you want to accomplish in your future. It's basically a visual representation of your hopes and dreams. Its purpose is to serve as inspiration and motivation for working toward your goals. Being able to see exactly what you want to accomplish gives you something to work toward as opposed to just letters on a page. Though there’s still value in writing your financial goals down, seeing is believing."
